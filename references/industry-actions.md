# Industry Actions

Use these action banks to customize the daily brief.

## 餐饮

- Morning: breakfast bundle, office preorder, community pickup.
- Noon: lunch set, nearby office worker discount, review incentive.
- Evening: family set, late-night combo, old customer bring-a-friend.
- Copy angle: "今天不用纠结吃什么".

## 美业

- Weekday: idle-time appointment discount, single-service trial.
- Weekend: party/date-ready package, before/after case sharing.
- Member action: referral reward, birthday care, package renewal reminder.
- Copy angle: "先体验，再决定是否办卡".

## 教培

- Weekday: assessment appointment, after-school trial class.
- Weekend: parent open class, learning plan consultation.
- Member action: old student referral, parent feedback note.
- Copy angle: "先看问题，再定方案".

## 健身

- Weekday: after-work trial, low-pressure beginner class.
- Weekend: body assessment, partner training card.
- Member action: check-in challenge, private coaching consultation.
- Copy angle: "先建立习惯，不急着拼强度".

## 零售

- Weekday: new-arrival notice, member-only item, bundle discount.
- Weekend: in-store try-on, limited shelf, clearance theme.
- Member action: points reminder, replenishment reminder.
- Copy angle: "顺路到店看看今天的新款".

## 母婴

- Weekday: replenishment reminder, parenting tip, age-based product list.
- Weekend: parent micro-class, baby milestone gift.
- Member action: neighborhood parent group, referral gift.
- Copy angle: "给新手爸妈少踩坑".

## 宠物

- Weekday: grooming appointment fill, pet photo sharing.
- Weekend: pet social day, boarding preview, birthday service.
- Member action: vaccine/grooming reminder, referral gift.
- Copy angle: "记录毛孩子今天的可爱".
