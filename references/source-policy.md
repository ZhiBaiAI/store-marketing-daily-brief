# Source Policy

## Prefer sources in this order

1. Official calendars and public holiday announcements.
2. Platform official pages, trend pages, creator centers, or advertising help centers.
3. Public search results from major local-life or social platforms.
4. Public news or industry reports.
5. Stable calendar-based signals when live access is unavailable.

## Required source note

Always include a short source note at the bottom.

When live sources were checked:

```text
信息整理来源：公开节日节点、平台公开趋势、行业营销日历及大可AI本地生活运营经验。实时热点仅基于公开信息整理，请以平台当前页面为准。
```

When no live sources were checked:

```text
信息整理来源：公开节日节点、通用行业营销日历及大可AI本地生活运营经验。本简报未接入实时热搜，仅供今日运营参考。
```

## Restricted topics

Do not recommend marketing around:

- disasters, accidents, death, illness outbreaks, violence, or public tragedy
- politics, military conflict, ethnic or religious conflict
- celebrity scandals involving crime, death, minors, or harassment
- medical claims, guaranteed treatment outcomes, or misleading health promises
- financial guarantees, investment promises, or illegal promotions

If a user asks to use these topics, redirect to neutral alternatives such as season, weekday, community life, family needs, weather, or store member benefits.
